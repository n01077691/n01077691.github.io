Welcome To Order vs Chaos

Order starts first. Its' task is to fill the table with line of 5 circles in any direction no matter what color

Chaos task is to prevent order in the table

PUSH HERE TO RESTART!

    STEPS TO PLAY A GAME:


1. Choose between you and 
your partner order or chaos.

2. Order starts, push on
a circle to confirm your color
(chosen cicrle lights up)

3. Place your circle in one
of the table cells by simply 
clicking on it.

4. Chaos would take next turn 
in same way (choose color, 
confirm position)

5. Order and Chaos switch 
turns until Order wins - gets
5 circles in line in any 
direction; OR when it has no 
options to achieve 5 circles 
in line. In that case, 
Chaos wins!
